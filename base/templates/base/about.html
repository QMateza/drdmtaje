Meet your trusted dentist

Dr. Ditlhare Taje

  • Over 15 years of dental experience
  • Bachelor of Science (BSc), 2003 – Medical University of Southern Africa (MEDUNSA)
  • Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS), 2010 – Medical University of Southern Africa (MEDUNSA)
  • Extensive experience serving diverse communities across the Lejweleputswa region
  • Former dental practitioner in Bloemfontein (Mangaung Metro) from 2010 to 2016

Dr. Taje, a committed Free State dentist, is recognized for her compassionate, patient-first approach. Through community outreach and dedicated care, she ensures every patient receives personalized, high-quality treatment in a welcoming environment.

We Accept All Major Medical Aids

At Dr DM Taje practice, we believe quality dental care should be accessible to everyone. That’s why we proudly accept all major medical aids, ensuring our services are convenient and affordable for you and your family. Our team is happy to assist with claims and guide you through the process, so you can focus on maintaining a healthy smile without added stress.

Caring Beyond Smiles

At Dr. DM Taje's practice, patients receive expert dental care in a warm, welcoming environment. The team is dedicated to personalized, high-quality service that ensures every visit is comfortable. Beyond the practice, Dr. Taje is passionate about community outreach such as visiting schools to teach children about oral health and hygiene.
